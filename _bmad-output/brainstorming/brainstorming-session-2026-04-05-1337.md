# Brainstorming Session Results

**Facilitator:** Itobeo
**Date:** 2026-04-05

## Session Overview

**Topic:** Redesigning PiggyMetrics UI for teenagers
**Goals:** Generate design concepts and UI/UX solutions for a teen-friendly experience

### Session Setup

Redesigning the PiggyMetrics financial advisor app to appeal to a teenage audience. Focus on visual design, UX patterns, tone, and interaction style that resonates with teens.

## Technique Selection

**Approach:** AI-Recommended Techniques
**Analysis Context:** Teen-friendly UI redesign with focus on concrete design outputs

**Recommended Techniques:**

- **Role Playing:** Embody teen personas to uncover needs, pain points, and desires before designing
- **SCAMPER Method:** Systematically apply 7 lenses to the current UI to generate concrete redesign ideas
- **Alien Anthropologist:** View the current UI through completely foreign eyes to surface hidden assumptions and unconventional directions

**AI Rationale:** Empathy-first approach (Role Playing) grounds ideas in real teen needs, SCAMPER generates systematic concrete ideas, and Alien Anthropologist pushes into novel territory beyond obvious solutions.


## Technique Execution Results

### Role Playing (45 ideas)
Embodied teen persona "Jordan" (16, saving for AirPods) through the full app journey — login to post-goal experience.

- **[Design #1]** Dark Mode First — Default dark mode with vibrant accents signals the app "gets" teens before they log in
- **[Design #2]** Gradient Login Screen — Animated gradient background replaces boring white form
- **[Design #3]** Social Login Only — Google/Apple/Discord login, zero friction, no password forms
- **[Design #4]** Piggy Mascot "Penny" — Animated pig character on login, reacts to interactions, celebrates success
- **[Design #5]** Goal-First Home Screen — Hero element is savings goal card with chunky progress bar, not balance number
- **[Design #6]** Penny as Financial Health Indicator — Penny's mood reflects financial health, sends gentle nudges
- **[Design #7]** Penny + Goal Combo Card — Penny sits on progress bar, moves closer to goal as savings grow
- **[Design #8]** Penny Chat Input — Tap Penny, type "bubble tea $6", Penny parses and logs it conversationally
- **[Design #9]** Penny's Reaction to Spending — Contextual insight at moment of logging: "3rd bubble tea this week 🧋"
- **[Design #10]** Smart Category Guessing — Auto-categorizes from keywords, one tap to fix if wrong
- **[Design #11]** Stories-Style Spending Summary — Weekly recap as swipeable story cards like Instagram Stories
- **[Design #12]** Profile Card with Financial Stats — Savings streak, total saved, Saver Level (Bronze→Legend)
- **[Design #13]** Shareable Savings Milestones — Auto-generated shareable card at 50% goal completion
- **[Design #14]** "For You" Finance Feed — Scrollable feed of personalized tips and saving challenges
- **[Design #15]** Daily Logging Streak — Log one transaction/day to keep streak; Penny devastated if broken
- **[Design #16]** Saving Challenges with Friends — "No Spend Weekend" leaderboard, winner gets Penny badge
- **[Design #17]** Penny Morning Nudge — Context-aware push: "3 days into streak 🔥 $12 away from AirPods"
- **[Design #18]** Weekly Penny Report — Sunday wrap framed positively even when spending was high
- **[Design #19]** Goal-First Onboarding — First screen: "What are you saving for?" Visual goal cards, no bank connection
- **[Design #20]** Penny's Instant Savings Plan — Enter price + date, Penny calculates: "Save $31/week → AirPods by June 15"
- **[Design #21]** Penny Introduction Moment — Penny appears after goal set: "Hi! I'm your saving buddy 🐷"
- **[Design #22]** "How do you get money?" Onboarding — Teen language: Allowance/Part-time job/Both instead of "income source"
- **[Design #23]** Skip Everything, Start Tracking — Skip all setup, land on home screen immediately
- **[Design #24]** Penny's Honest Bestie Voice — Copy rule: "what would a supportive older sibling say?"
- **[Design #25]** Contextual Penny Reactions — ~20 distinct message templates for specific events, never feels automated
- **[Design #26]** Penny Never Says "Budget" — Banned word. Replace with "your plan," "what you've got left"
- **[Design #27]** Penny's Slang Calibration — Settings: Chill 😌 / Normal 🙂 / Extra 🔥 tone modes
- **[Design #28]** "What If" Simulator — Slider: "Cut bubble tea in half → save $36 extra → AirPods 3 weeks sooner"
- **[Design #29]** "Skip It" Button — Penny notices recurring spend, offers one-tap redirect to goal fund
- **[Design #30]** Goal Countdown Mode — Within $30 of goal: UI changes color, Penny gets excited, big countdown number
- **[Design #31]** Penny's Weekly Roast (opt-in) — Funny brutally honest Sunday recap, screenshot-worthy
- **[Design #32]** Emoji Spending Breakdown — 🧋🧋🧋🧋🧋 vs 🍟🍟 — visual comparison without reading numbers
- **[Design #33]** "Rewind" Progress Card — "30 days ago: $12 saved. Today: $87. That's 7x more 🚀"
- **[Design #34]** Spending Personality of the Week — "Snack Goblin 🍟", "Saving Legend 💰" — shareable sticker card
- **[Design #35]** "Glow Up" Stats View — Default shows improvement over time, not raw numbers. Raw data one tap away
- **[Design #36]** "Every Penny Counts" Micro-Saving — No minimum. Celebrate every $0.50 addition explicitly
- **[Design #37]** No Balance Required — Works purely on manual logs, zero bank connection, zero scary permissions
- **[Design #38]** "Saving from Zero" Mode — Empty state: "Everyone starts at zero. Even Bezos did. Log your first $1 🐷"
- **[Design #39]** Flexible Goal Types — "Just saving," "Emergency fund," "No idea yet" — vague goals are valid
- **[Design #40]** Penny-Voiced Navigation — "My Stuff" / "My Journey" / Penny face / "Penny Says" / "My Vibe"
- **[Design #41]** Penny Face as Primary CTA — Center nav = Penny's animated face, blinks idle, bounces when new content
- **[Design #42]** "My Vibe" Settings — Leads with personalization; functional settings buried under "Boring Stuff ⚙️"

- **[Design #44]** Penny's "What's Next?" Email — 24hr post-goal: 3 suggested next goals based on spending patterns

### SCAMPER Method (26 ideas)
Systematic 7-lens transformation of the current PiggyMetrics UI.

- **[Design #46]** Custom Emoji Account Names — 🎧 AirPods, 🧋 Drinks, 👟 Sneaker Fund — emoji appears everywhere
- **[Design #47]** "Money In / Money Out" Language — Replace all Income/Expense terminology app-wide
- **[Design #48]** Goal + Stats Combined View — Each goal has its own mini-dashboard, swipe between like stories
- **[Design #49]** Penny Chat + Expense Log Combined — Full expense history + logging in one conversational thread
- **[Design #50]** Streak + Goal Progress Combined — Streak feeds goal bar visually; breaking streak sets back goal
- **[Design #51]** Onboarding + First Goal Setup Combined — By end of onboarding, goal set + Penny met + first transaction logged
- **[Design #52]** Duolingo Streak Shame (Adapted) — Penny looks genuinely sad when streak breaks: "I missed you 🐷💔"
- **[Design #53]** Penny Wrapped — Annual shareable year-in-review in Instagram Stories format
- **[Design #54]** Snapchat Streaks (Adapted) — Social saving streaks between friends; breaking it affects a relationship
- **[Design #55]** BeReal Honesty Moment — Random-timed daily "Penny Check!" — log what you spent in last hour
- **[Design #56]** Micro Goals — Auto-break big goals into weekly targets. $249 → "Save $31 this week"
- **[Design #57]** Massive Celebration Animations — Full-screen takeover for every milestone, unskippable 2 seconds
- **[Design #58]** Simplified Statistics — Default: exactly 3 numbers. Money In / Money Out / Saved toward goal
- **[Design #59]** Penny Size Reflects Savings — Penny grows physically on screen as total savings increase
- **[Design #60]** Penny as Financial Tutor — Notification system repurposed for weekly 1-sentence money tips
- **[Design #61]** Spending History as Story Archive — Transaction history reframed as "Your Money Story" timeline
- **[Design #62]** Statistics as Goal Ammunition — Every stat has "What if I cut this?" button feeding the simulator
- **[Design #63]** Savings Goals as Family Wishlist — Share goals with family; grandma can contribute $20 to AirPods goal
- **[Design #64]** Kill the Currency Selector — Remove from main UI, hide in settings for edge cases
- **[Design #65]** Kill the Account Structure — Replace accounts with flat goals list + one spending tracker
- **[Design #66]** Kill Empty Onboarding Screens — Remove every screen that doesn't result in Jordan doing something
- **[Design #67]** Kill the Settings Complexity — Max 5 settings options visible
- **[Design #68]** Reverse the Flow — Log spending freely, Penny reflects back weekly. No upfront planning
- **[Design #69]** Reverse the Reward — Celebrate restraint (not spending) as much as saving
- **[Design #70]** Reverse the Goal Direction — "You need $162 more" countdown vs. "You have $87 saved" count-up
- **[Design #71]** Reverse the Privacy — Saving goals public to friend group by default; private is opt-in

### Alien Anthropologist (6 ideas)
Viewed PiggyMetrics through completely foreign eyes to surface hidden assumptions.

- **[Design #72]** Penny as Actual Advisor — Proactively tells Jordan what to do next, not just what happened
- **[Design #73]** Zero-Input Expense Logging — Penny reads bank SMS/payment notifications, auto-logs expenses
- **[Design #74]** Penny Learns Jordan's Patterns — After 30 days, hyper-personalized suggestions based on behavior
- **[Design #75]** Social Status as Success Metric — "Saver Rep" score visible to friends; financial behavior = social currency
- **[Design #76]** Penny Lives on the Lock Screen — Lock screen widget: goal progress + streak + one Penny message
- **[Design #77]** Rebrand the App's Identity — Teen version called "Penny" or "Goals" — mascot/outcome, not function

## Idea Organization and Prioritization

### Thematic Clusters

**🐷 Penny the Mascot System** (#4, #6, #7, #17, #21, #24, #25, #26, #27, #41, #42, #59, #72, #74, #77)
The mascot as emotional core — mood indicator, chat interface, advisor, brand identity, and retention mechanic.

**🎯 Goal-First Experience** (#5, #19, #20, #22, #23, #43, #44, #56, #70)
Redesigning the entire app around goals rather than accounts/budgets.

**📱 Teen-Native UI Language** (#2, #3, #11, #12, #13, #14, #32, #33, #34, #35, #40, #46, #47)
Social media aesthetics, stories format, emoji-based data, shareable cards, Penny-voiced navigation.

**🔁 Retention & Habit Loops** (#15, #16, #18, #52, #53, #54, #55, #75, #76)
Streaks, social challenges, Penny Wrapped, BeReal-style check-ins, lock screen presence.

**⚡ Friction Elimination** (#8, #9, #10, #37, #38, #64, #65, #66, #67, #73)
Zero-input logging, social login, killing jargon, removing onboarding dead zones, auto-categorization.

**🔮 Killer Features** (#28, #29, #30, #31, #60, #61, #62, #63)
What If simulator, Skip It button, Penny's Weekly Roast, Penny Wrapped, family wishlist.

**♿ Inclusion & Accessibility** (#36, #37, #38, #39)
No minimums, no bank connection required, empty state design, vague goals supported.

### Prioritization Results

**Top 3 High-Impact Ideas:**
- **#4 + #72** — Penny as mascot + actual advisor (unified concept — the heart of the redesign)
- **#28** — "What If" Simulator (the killer feature teens would talk about)
- **#19 + #20** — Goal-first onboarding + Penny's instant savings plan

**Quick Win Opportunities** (low effort, high visible impact):
- **#47** — Replace "Income/Expense" with "Money In/Money Out" — copy change only
- **#40** — Penny-voiced navigation labels — copy change only
- **#46** — Emoji account names — small UI addition
- **#66** — Kill passive onboarding screens — removal, not building

**Breakthrough Concepts** (longer-term, high innovation):
- **#73** — Zero-input expense logging via bank notifications
- **#63** — Savings goals as family wishlist
- **#53** — Penny Wrapped annual recap
